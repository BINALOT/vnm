MISSION

At Binalot sa Tondo, we're dedicated to celebrating the rich flavors of Tondo while providing exceptional dining experiences. Inspired by Lyka Monreal's leadership, we craft dishes that blend tradition with innovation, ensuring every guest enjoys authentic flavors and memorable moments. Rooted in our community, we strive to contribute to its vitality and cultural richness, making Binalot sa Tondo a cherished destination for food lovers and a proud symbol of Filipino culinary heritage.

VISSION

Our vision at Binalot sa Tondo is to become the epitome of Filipino culinary excellence, celebrated both locally and globally. Inspired by the innovative spirit of Lyka Monreal, we aim to continuously push boundaries, delighting our customers with inventive dishes that pay homage to our heritage. We envision a future where Binalot sa Tondo serves as a beacon of culinary innovation, fostering community pride and enriching the cultural tapestry of Tondo and beyond.

Core Values

Our core values at Binalot sa Tondo drive our mission. We prioritize Culinary Excellence, Authenticity, Innovation, Community Engagement, and Customer Satisfaction. We're committed to serving top-quality dishes rooted in Filipino tradition, embracing creativity, and actively contributing to our community's growth while ensuring exceptional service for every guest.
